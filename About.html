ABOUT US:

Ahbaabul Khairiya, in abbreviation ‘AKH’ is an Islamic foundation (NGO) which deals with helping widowers and orphans who are living vulnerably life, home bounded patients who can’t afford the treatment expenses, and old people who doesn’t have care taker, especially those who are living in the remote villages. Ahbaabul Khairiya is permitted by the government of Tanzania to performed there work in five regions including Dar es Salaam region which is its headquarters. Others region are Coastal, Morogoro, Tanga and Dodoma.




AHBAABUL KHAIRIYA is a non-political religious organization designed on the basis of the Holy Qur'an and the Sunna of the Prophet Muhammad S.A.W. was established by 15 Members in September 2015 and come into operation in May 2016 after submitting request to operate in the country to the Ministry of Home Affairs
AKH duly registered by the Ministry of Home Affairs No SA 18233 is situated at Kinondoni District, Mwananyamala Kisukari street house No 152 nearby Masjid Hidaya.



AKH performance is based on donations (Charity and Zakka) from Islamic believers and others. It provides services in the areas of health, education and economy as main services. We pay school fees, fares from and to schools and school supplies to orphans at the end of each year, pay house rent for widows with housing problems. Provide, food and medical care for patients with Aids, cancer, TB and other diseases. We also provide capital for widows with small business capital as Allah wishes.
During the holy month of Ramadan we have a great need of providing widows families with iftaar suhoor and Iddi el Fitri new clothes.
We kindly request you to join us in looking at our society that needs our collective power. We hope your compassionate eye will look after them.



OUR GOALS AND OBJECTIVES:
a)To care for widows who have lost their husbands, divorced, and unmarried for long-term, To provide basic humanitarian needs, medical, financial and business training;
b)To provide a conducive environment for the implementation of Islamic life systems in various fields such as Education, Worship, Health, Da’wa, Economy and its culture;
c)Supporting communities in trouble and comfort and economic empowerment;
d)To care for Orphans, Prisoners and Households who are unable to afford medical expenses;
e)To facilitate the construction of mosques, Building Qur’an madrasas and drilling fresh water wells;
f)Facilitate the construction of world education centers, pre-primary schools, primary, secondary and vocational education and vocational training;
g)Receiving and giving gifts, grants, loans, Zaka from or to institutions, government, individuals in and out of the country if such items are in line with sharia and Islamic religious systems and the laws of the country
h)Establish and develop livestock, agricultural and other projects;
i)To distribute the holy Qur’an, and papers with good moral values, religious books, and to show positive movies for the community and the general public.
